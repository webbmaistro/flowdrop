### 2. Zapier (Most Popular)

**What it does**: Connects Google Sheets to over 5,000 apps using "Zaps" (their word for workflows).

**The catch**: You have to build each step by hand. Pick your trigger, add actions, map fields. It works, but there's a learning curve.

**Best for**: People who don't mind watching how-to videos and have time to set things up.

**Pricing**: Free plan is very basic. Paid plans start at $20/month.

---

### 3. Make (Formerly Integromat)

**What it does**: Power tool for complex work. You can build really fancy stuff with loops, filters, and logic.

**The catch**: Even more complex than Zapier. You're building visual flows with nodes and paths.

**Best for**: Teams with someone tech-savvy who can set things up for everyone else.

**Pricing**: Free tier exists. Paid plans start at $9/month.

---

### 4. Coefficient

**What it does**: Pulls live data from other tools into your Google Sheet. Think sales data, customer info, all updating on its own.

**The catch**: Great for getting data IN, but not as good at taking action based on what's there.

**Best for**: Teams who live in sheets and want fresh data without export/import.

**Pricing**: Free plan available. Paid starts around $49/month.
